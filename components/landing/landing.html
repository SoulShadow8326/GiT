Girls in Tech 2025 is here!
The technology club of DPS R.K. Puram, Exun Clan, proudly presents its annual Girls in Tech, a fast–paced, high–intensity tech event designed to push creativity, strategy, and problem-solving to the limit.

Teams will compete in a rapid-fire series of technical challenges spanning coding, logic, design thinking, and real-time decision making — all compressed into an electrifying 4-hour sprint.

GiT 2025 brings a refined format, tougher challenges, and an elevated competitive environment.

The 6-hour event will run from 6:00 PM IST to 12:00 AM IST on 19th November 2025.
